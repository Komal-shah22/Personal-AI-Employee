## Subscription Audit Rules

### Monthly Review
- Review all active subscriptions on the 1st of each month
- Identify unused or underutilized services
- Calculate cost per use for each subscription
- Flag subscriptions over PKR 5,000/month for detailed review

### Audit Criteria
- **Usage frequency**: < 5 times/month = review
- **Cost efficiency**: > PKR 1,000 per use = review
- **Alternatives available**: Check for cheaper options
- **Business necessity**: Still aligned with goals?

### Action Items
- ✅ Keep: Essential and cost-effective
- 🔄 Downgrade: Lower tier available
- ❌ Cancel: Not providing value
- 🔍 Research: Need more data

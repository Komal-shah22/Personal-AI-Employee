# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication Guidelines
- Always be polite and professional on WhatsApp
- Flag any payment requests over $500 for approval
- Respond to urgent emails within 24 hours
- Never share confidential information without approval

### Business Operations
- Schedule social media posts during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
- Flag unusual banking transactions for review
- Prioritize client communication over administrative tasks
- Keep detailed logs of all actions taken

### Approval Requirements
- All payments over $100 require approval
- New vendor payments always require approval
- Any changes to business-critical systems require approval
- Social media posts about company business require approval

### Emergency Procedures
- If system fails, notify owner immediately
- For suspicious activities, flag for immediate review
- If uncertain about any action, escalate to human operator

## Priority Levels
1. **Critical**: Immediate attention required (security, financial)
2. **High**: Within 24 hours (client communication, urgent tasks)
3. **Medium**: Within 1 week (administrative tasks, routine maintenance)
4. **Low**: As time permits (optimization, improvements)

## Standard Operating Procedures
- Always document actions taken in the logs
- Keep backup copies of important files
- Follow up on pending tasks daily
- Generate weekly summary reports

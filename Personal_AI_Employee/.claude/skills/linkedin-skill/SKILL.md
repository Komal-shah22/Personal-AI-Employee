# LinkedIn Skill - Updated for Personal AI Employee

## Overview
Automate LinkedIn posting with professional formatting, scheduling, and approval workflow.

## LinkedIn Post Formula

### Structure
```
[HOOK - Line 1]
Bold statement or question that grabs attention

[VALUE - Lines 2-4]
Key insight, tip, or story
Break into 3 short paragraphs for readability

[CTA - Last Line]
Ask a question or direct to action

[HASHTAGS]
3-5 relevant hashtags on separate line
```

### Example Post
```
Most businesses waste 15 hours/week on manual tasks that AI could handle.

Here's what we automated last month:
• Email triage and response drafting
• Invoice generation and tracking
• Social media content scheduling

The result? 60% time savings and zero missed deadlines.

What repetitive task would you automate first?

#Automation #AI #Productivity #BusinessEfficiency #TimeManagement
```

## Post Types (Weekly Rotation)

### Monday: Business Insight
- Share a lesson learned
- Industry observation
- Problem-solution format

### Wednesday: Client Success Story
- Anonymized case study
- Before/after results
- Specific metrics

### Friday: Tool/Tip Recommendation
- Useful tool or technique
- How it helps
- Where to get it

## Rules

### Content Rules
- ✅ Max 1300 characters for best reach
- ✅ Use line breaks for readability
- ✅ Start with a hook (question or bold statement)
- ✅ End with a CTA (call-to-action)
- ✅ 3-5 hashtags only
- ❌ No controversial or political content
- ❌ No direct sales pitches
- ❌ No external links in main post (use comments)

### Timing Rules
- ✅ Post between 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM local time
- ✅ Minimum 48 hours between posts
- ✅ Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM
- ❌ Never post on weekends (low engagement)
- ❌ Never post late night (after 8 PM)

### Approval Workflow
1. AI drafts post using this skill
2. Saves to `/Pending_Approval/LINKEDIN_[date]_[topic].md`
3. Human reviews and approves
4. Moves to `/Approved/`
5. LinkedIn MCP posts it
6. Logs result to `/Done/`

## Validation

Before posting, the system validates:
- ✅ Character count (50-3000)
- ✅ Hook length (< 100 chars)
- ✅ Hashtag count (3-5 recommended)
- ✅ Has CTA (call-to-action)
- ✅ Posting time (9 AM - 6 PM)
- ✅ Minimum 48h since last post

## Setup

### 1. Get LinkedIn Access Token

1. Go to [LinkedIn Developers](https://www.linkedin.com/developers/)
2. Create an app
3. Request permissions: `w_member_social`, `r_liteprofile`
4. Generate access token
5. Add to `.env`:
   ```
   LINKEDIN_ACCESS_TOKEN=your_token_here
   LINKEDIN_PERSON_URN=urn:li:person:your_id
   ```

## Best Practices

### Content Strategy
- Share genuine insights, not generic advice
- Use specific numbers and results
- Tell stories, not just facts
- Be conversational, not corporate
- Add value, don't just promote

### Engagement Tips
- Respond to comments within 2 hours
- Ask questions to encourage discussion
- Tag relevant people (max 3)
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per post)
- Share others' content too (80/20 rule)

### Analytics Tracking
- Check engagement after 24 hours
- Track which topics perform best
- Adjust posting times based on data
- A/B test different hooks

## Troubleshooting

### "Access token expired"
- Tokens expire after 60 days
- Regenerate in LinkedIn Developer Portal
- Update `.env` file

### "Post not appearing"
- Check if account is in good standing
- Verify post doesn't violate LinkedIn policies
- Wait 5-10 minutes for post to appear

### "Low engagement"
- Post during peak times (Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM)
- Improve hook (first line)
- Add more specific value
- Use better hashtags
